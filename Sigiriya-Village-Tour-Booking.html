Sigiria Village Tour summary

Embark on a 2.5-hour journey that offers a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to immerse yourself in the nature and culture of a genuine Sri Lankan village. A driver will pick you up from your hotel in Sigiriya or Dambulla and take you by Tuk Tuk to Ehhelagala Village, a picturesque rural area in the city of Sigiriya.

The tour begins with a ride on a traditional Bullock cart near the village pond. Here, you will embark on a boat ride that will take you to the other side of the rural village. After a brief stroll through the paddy fields and vegetable farms, you will arrive at a village house.

Here, you can unwind and savor some delectable Sri Lankan snacks. Following this, you will hop back onto the Tuk Tuk and ride back to the starting point of the tour. Once the tour concludes, you will be taken back to your hotel by Tuk Tuk. This tour offers a unique glimpse into the heart of Sri Lankan rural life.

Itinerary

Hotel pickup by Tuk tuk: 10.30 AM (Flexible)

You will be picked up from your hotel and drive approximately 15-30 minutes to reach the trekking starting point. Short transfer gives you a very good chance to experience the natural breeze and take photos of the attractions alongside the road.

Bullock cart ride: 11.00 pm

Usually this trek begins with a Bullock Cart Ride to Ehelagala through unbeaten village tracks. It adjacent to the great ‘Sigiriya rock’. You can ride the bullock cart in gravel path becomes the dust, red soil of a bund. There are 2 Bulls dragging the cart and ride last long from 15 minutes. Rides ends near the Lake.

Canoe Boat ride

Canoe Boat Ride takes place on the Ehelagala lake. You will cruise admiring the amazing views besides the lake which is also overlooking the Sigiriya and pidurangala rocks. If you are interested in, you can also join with the Boatman and help out rowing the boat. He might surprise you with his craftsmanship and creativity, hat for the gentleman and a beautiful necklace for the ladies. At the end of the Tour Boat will dock by the small village on the other side of the shore.

Village House & Lunch / Snacks: 11.45 pm – 1.00pm

You can observe a traditional daily live of a real village house. Should not miss a visit to the paddy field, See a garden where endemic vegetable grown. Climb the tree house next to the paddy field where farmers sleeps at night and try to keep animals away from the paddy.

You can enjoy a simple traditional meal prepared by a village woman the using the Farmer’s hand-picked vegetables from the plot. If time permits you can also see how a village woman cook a traditional meal or a roti.

After lunch / Snacks, there will be small presentation of how to make a roof out of coconut leaves and how to use kulla and mirisgala to separate rice seeds from the husks and straw.

Tuk Tuk ride: 1.00 pm

At the end of the village tour you will be taking a Tuk-Tuk ride back to the starting point.

Hotel drop-off by Tuk Tuk: 1.15 PM

Then drive back to your hotel by safari jeep.

Arrive your hotel: 1.30 pm
