  • Activities
    Whale and dolphin watching

    Sri Lanka, a hotspot for dolphin and whale watching, offers unforgettable encounters with these majestic creatures. Witness the spectacle in Mirissa, Trincomalee, or Kalpitiya. The best time to visit is from November to April. Experience the thrill of nature’s grandeur.

    Hot air baloon

    Experience the thrill of a hot air balloon ride in Sri Lanka. Soar over the cultural triangle, witness lush green jungles, spectacular peaks, and peaceful green fields. Drift up to 2000 feet, feel almost motionless as you move with the wind. A unique adventure that leaves you with unforgettable memories of Sri Lanka’s stunning landscapes

    Sigiria vilage tour

    Embark on a Sigiriya Village Tour for an authentic Sri Lankan experience. Traverse the village in a bullock cart, sail over a reservoir on a catamaran, and learn about traditional agriculture. Enjoy a cooking demonstration and savor a local lunch. A journey into the heart of Sri Lanka’s rural life awaits you
